The Kings Gambit

The King's Gambit has a long and storied history and was a favorite opening of many top players in the 19th century. However, as chess theory evolved, players began to find ways to neutralize White's aggressive intentions.

Today, it is less frequently played at the highest levels of chess,
but it remains a popular choice among players seeking dynamic and tactical games..
